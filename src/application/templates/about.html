About us:

the Working Computer is a (very) small company dedicated, as the little banner
below suggests, to making computers work for people. This happens via IT and web services,
and products for individuals with traumatic brain injuries or developmental disabilities.
We also make web, mobile, and desktop applications in other areas we find interesting,
such as music, mathematics, logistics, and computer gaming.

Technology:

We like Python! and we use it for everything we can, including this
WebSite (Flask).We also work in Clojure, Android, and Javascript, and are considering
the merits of porting to the Iphone/Ipad.

We work and live in New Hampshire, USA.
